Waste Segregation at a Glance

Organic

Food scraps, peels, garden leaves. Route to compost systems.

Recyclable

Paper, metal cans, glass, clean plastic. Keep dry and sorted.

Hazardous

Batteries, chemicals, e-waste. Use marked hazardous bins only.

Residual

Contaminated wrappers and non-recyclables. Minimize this stream.
