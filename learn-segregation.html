🗑️ Know Your Trash: The Segregation Guide

Sort better, recover more, and keep campus spaces healthy and clean.

🍏 Organic Waste (Mint Bin)

What it is: Biodegradable matter that naturally breaks down.

Examples:
  • Fruit peels
  • Leftover food
  • Tea leaves
  • Garden branches

♻️ Recyclable Waste (Pastel Blue Bin)

What it is: Materials that can be processed and used again.

Examples:
  • Clean paper
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Plastic bottles
  • Glass jars
  • Metal cans

⚠️ Hazardous Waste (Soft Coral/Pink Bin)

What it is: Toxic, flammable, or corrosive materials that need special handling.

Examples:
  • Used batteries
  • E-waste (old wires, broken mice)
  • Lab chemicals

🗑️ Residual Waste (Dusty Lilac Bin)

What it is: Mixed waste that cannot be recycled or composted.

Examples:
  • Chip wrappers
  • Sanitary products
  • Dirty tissue papers
